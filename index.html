Essential stuff to put in your go bag

Instructions on which things to put in emergency go bags

The important things are:

Canned Food

We need canned food in go bags because it provides a long-lasting, non-perishable, and nutrient-rich food source that is easy to prepare during emergencies. This is crucial when fresh food is unavailable and cooking facilities may be out of service, allowing you to sustain yourself until help arrives or you reach a safer location.

First Aid Kit

First aid is needed in go bags because emergency situations can cause injuries, and a go bag allows you to provide immediate care to prevent a situation from worsening while waiting for professional help.

Electronics

Electronics are essential in a go bag primarily for communication, staying informed, and accessing important information during and after a disaster, when normal infrastructure like electricity and internet may be unavailable.

Clothes

Packing clothes in a go bag (or emergency kit) is essential for protection from the elements, maintaining hygiene, comfort, and potential physical activity during an emergency evacuation, which may last for at least 72 hours.
